Our History


My Piano Teacher

I really love playing the piano simply because I had the best teacher ever! His name was Alf Townsend, many of his students have gone on to great things and have taken different paths, but to the best of my knowledge we are all still playing the piano because we love to!

I also study regularly to improve my playing and musical knowledge, the skills gained are then passed on to my own students. I believe in continuous improvement and offering my very best to others.

Our Story

Our story began not with quiet practice, but with the energetic hum of a recording studio. This space was once alive with aspiring musicians and established artists, creating a vibrant tapestry of sound. Over time, we felt a new calling: to share the joy of music in a more personal way. Inspired by our students, we transformed this studio into a haven for piano learners. With careful planning, we exchanged microphones for music stands, welcoming students of all ages and levels to embark on their musical journeys.
