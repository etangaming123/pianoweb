Ethan's Composition

Ethan has always had a passion for creation, whether it be games on Scratch, Python projects, Blender projects, or Roblox animations. However, nothing stood out more than his own musical creation titled "I Can Be Here"! He played this in my studio and I was blown away, not just by his peformance, but by how simple yet amazing this song sounds!

"I was in Japan at new years in 2022, and me and my family saw this store named 'Why are you here?', so we decided to take a photo, and relooking at the photo I decided to make a song titled 'I Can Be Here'!" -Ethan
