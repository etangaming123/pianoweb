Frequently Asked Questions


What piano do I play on?

Lessons are taught on an upright Kawai piano, however a grand piano is avaliable for more advanced students.

Can you print a piano piece? 

With the availability of music online, we can obtain virtually any pieces of music ever composed in any genre. Please let me know and I will obtain the music and be prepared for you.

When are you open? 

We are open Monday to Friday 10 am to 9pm and all day on Saturdays.
