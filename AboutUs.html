Our Memories

We've been around since 2007, how the time flies! Since that time, we have taught many amazing students and welcomed many fantastic artists, from Aria nominees to Aria Hall of Fame Inductees! We are proud of our studio, and we look forward to our future!

Our Piano

Steve's Bio

Steve began playing piano in the early 1970s and started auditioning for bands at age seventeen. His first band was Victims of Gravity, where he played alongside Hue Munroe and even appeared in a music video with John St. Peters. He later formed FingerPop with musicians including Daryl Green and James Grant.
